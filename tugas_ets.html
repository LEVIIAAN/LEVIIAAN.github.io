About Us

Casio is not just a timepiece; it is a testament to Japanese engineering excellence. Since 1946, we have pushed the boundaries of what a watch can be fusing indestructible materials with advanced digital intelligence.

  • The Gold Standard of Durability: Pioneering the shock-resistant structure that redefined toughness for generations.
  • Precision Technology: Implementing Multi-Band 6 and Bluetooth® synchronization for the most accurate timekeeping on the planet.
  • Sustainable Power: Leading the way with Tough Solar technology, converting even the weakest light into reliable energy.

From the deep ocean to the highest peaks, Casio is built to accompany those who challenge their limits. Our commitment to "Creativity and Contribution" ensures that every product we create adds value to your life and style.
